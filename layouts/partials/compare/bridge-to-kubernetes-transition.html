Concerned about making the transition from Bridge to Kubernetes?

We’ve got you covered. mirrord offers everything you’re used to with Bridge to Kubernetes and more.

Same approach, simpler setup

If you're used to the configuration processes in B2K, you'll appreciate how mirrord simplifies connecting your local process to any Kubernetes cluster—without the need for complex setup. Simply download one of the mirrord IDE extensions or the CLI tool and you’re good to go!

More customization, no extra effort

mirrord offers more control and flexibility over how your local and remote environments interact. You can choose exactly what your process does remotely and what happens locally - whether it’s the files it reads, environment variables, or even which hosts/IPs it connects to locally and or on the cluster.

Similar process, more use cases

You can maintain your usual development workflow, even if working with complex or high-scale Kubernetes setups. While B2K limits traffic redirection to single-pod, single-container services, mirrord enables seamless operation across multi-pod deployments, allows multiple developers to work in the same cluster concurrently, and efficiently handles strict network policies by routing outgoing traffic from the local process through the remote pod.
