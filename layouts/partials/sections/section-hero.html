*What’s Remocal?

Remote + Local = Remocal.

mirrord makes your local development experience feel 100% remote by making the running process think it's running in Staging. That means when you run your code with mirrord, it's the same as running it in Staging, without having to move your code arround, 'git push' to test, or do anything more than work directly from your CLI or IDE as you always have.
